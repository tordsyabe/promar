About Promar Shipping
Services

Promar Shipping Services SA was incorporated in 2002 with head office in Geneva as an independent organization specialising in ship operations.

Based on experiences with vessels chartering and the maintenance business in the oil industry, Promar Shipping Services SA naturally headed for managing offshore vessels, practising the same policy of high Standards of quality and Safety and keeping in mind the particular expectations and needs of the offshore oil and gas industry.

Thanks to a knowledgeable staff with broad expertise in the management of offshore vessels, Promar Shipping Services SA ensures the supervision of the fleet.

The fully dedicated and qualified personnel cover all the various competencies of the Ship Management business. All the services provided including QHSE, chartering, operations, crewing, technical, purchasing, insurances services, focus on customer satisfaction, charterer expectations and are guided by marine safety and safe working practices.

QHSE

Quality, Health, Safety, Environment

Promar Shipping Services SA is committed to the respect of quality, health, safety and environment, in the interest of its clients, partners and employees.

The highest standard level in the offshore industry and the best practices form an integral part of the Quality, Health, Safety and Environment culture and operational process. They are raised as the principles of the QHSE Management system.

To operate ships with the overriding priority of safeguarding the crew, preserving the integrity of the vessel and her cargo and protecting the environment from marine pollution.

To promote both on-board and on-shore a pervasive culture of safety, quality and environmental protection with the objective of systematically controlling all identified risks and maintaining constant readiness for emergencies.

To capitalize on competencies by providing continuous training for office and crew personnel.

To monitor, ensure, promote and guarantee the respect of the established policy on Quality, Health, Safety and Environmental protection.

To ensure a reciprocal respect between sea and shore staffs to promote harmony.

Serve quality offshore operations and contribute to client reputation and cost efficiency.

To set and maintain ethical standards in handling the affairs and interests of customers, and fair and correct in conducting relations with suppliers and contractors.
